# School Management App - Features & Functionality Requirements

## Overview

Comprehensive feature set covering all aspects of school management operations, from core academic functions to specialized services and administrative tools.

**📋 Reference**: See [Access Control Framework](./access-control.md) for complete role hierarchy and clearance level definitions.

## Core Academic Features

### Student Management

- **Student Records**: Complete student profiles with academic, personal, and medical information
- **Enrollment Management**: Registration, transfers, withdrawals, and graduation tracking
- **Academic History**: Transcripts, course history, and achievement records
- **Parent/Guardian Management**: Family relationships and contact information
- **Document Management**: Digital storage and retrieval of student documents

### Academic Management

- **Course Catalog**: Course creation, management, and scheduling
- **Subject Management**: Subject areas, prerequisites, and curriculum mapping
- **Class Scheduling**: Timetable creation, room assignment, and conflict resolution
- **Teacher Assignment**: Faculty allocation to courses and classes
- **Academic Calendar**: Terms, semesters, holidays, and important dates

### Grade & Assessment Management

- **Grade Entry**: Teacher grade input with validation and approval workflows
- **Assessment Tools**: Quizzes, tests, projects, and assignment tracking
- **Transcript Generation**: Official academic records and certificates
- **Progress Tracking**: Student performance monitoring and analytics
- **Report Cards**: Automated report generation and distribution

### Attendance Management

- **Daily Attendance**: Class-by-class attendance tracking
- **Absence Management**: Excuse processing and notification systems
- **Tardiness Tracking**: Late arrival monitoring and reporting
- **Attendance Reports**: Statistical analysis and compliance reporting
- **Parent Notifications**: Automated absence and tardiness alerts

## Administrative Features

### Financial Management

- **Fee Structure**: Tuition, fees, and payment plan management
- **Payment Processing**: Online payments, invoicing, and receipt generation
- **Financial Aid**: Scholarship and grant management
- **Billing Management**: Automated billing and payment reminders
- **Financial Reporting**: Revenue, expenses, and financial analytics

### Staff Management

- **Employee Records**: Staff profiles, qualifications, and employment history
- **Payroll Integration**: Salary management and payment processing
- **Performance Management**: Evaluations, goals, and professional development
- **Department Management**: Organizational structure and reporting relationships
- **HR Functions**: Leave management, benefits, and compliance tracking

### Communication Systems

- **Messaging Platform**: Internal communication between staff, students, and parents
- **Announcements**: School-wide and targeted announcements
- **Email Integration**: Automated email notifications and newsletters
- **SMS Notifications**: Text message alerts for urgent communications
- **Parent Portal**: Dedicated parent access to student information
