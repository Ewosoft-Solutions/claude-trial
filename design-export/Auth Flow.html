Auth flow · one login, every workspace

The unified entry model for SchoolWithEase: a single gateway → compulsory 2FA → workspace discovery. There's no open signup — owners arrive through two gated routes (managed demo ~80% / instant sandbox ~20%) and are provisioned only after compliance verification, while staff & families come by invite / import. Every tab is live.
